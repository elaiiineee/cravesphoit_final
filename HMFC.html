    Part 1 - Brine

    1. Wash chicken wings under cold water and pick out the feathers - place in a colander to drain
    2. Into a bowl, add in the brine ingredients. Stir till combined
    3. Add chicken, mix them together
    4. Cover with cling wrap, place it in the fridge then let it marinate for a minimum of 3 hours

    Part 2 - Frying Chicken

    1. Pull the wings out of the fridge
    2. Add oil to a large pot and heat it up

      3. **Try to keep it medium - high heat

    3. Add cornflour to a dry bowl
    4. In a separate bowl, add all seasoned flour ingredients then mixed till combined
    5. Check on oil to see if itâ€™s hot enough. Once itâ€™s hot make the wet batter

      6. *I use the chopstick method to know if the oil is hot enough

    6. For the wet batter, add 3 eggs + flour into a bowl and mix until just combined
    7. Slowly add in the Soda water, gently mix

      8. You donâ€™t want to lose the bubbles

    8. Set up your production line

      9. *1st Coat, Wet Batter then Seasoned Flour
      **Pro Tip: Shake off the excess you want a crispy coating not a bready one

    9. Fry chicken on medium high heat until batter is golden brown (~15-20mins)

    Part 3 - Honey Mustard Glaze

    1. While the chicken is frying melt the butter in a separate pot
    2. Add in mustard, honey and dark brown sugar
    3. Once the sugar has dissolved turn off the heat and use a brush to coat the chicken
