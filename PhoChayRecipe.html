Ingredients

Part 1 - Broth

  • Banh Pho (Rice Noodle)
  • 3L Hot Water
  • 5 Shiitake Mushrooms
  • 1 Leek
  • 1 Daikon (radish)
  • 1 Carrot
  • 1 Apple
  • 1 Nashi Pear
  • 2 Shallots
  • 1 Onion
  • Ginger
  • 1 Clump of Rock Sugar
  • Sea Salt
  • Soy Sauce
  • 2 Cardamom pods
  • 5 Star Anise
  • 4 Cloves
  • 2 Cinnamon sticks

Part 2 - Veggie/Tofu Stir-Fry

  • Sesame Oil
  • Hard Tofu
  • Chinese Broccoli (cut in ¬Ω)
  • Mushrooms (Enoki, Oyster etc)

Part 3 - Garnishes

  • Bean Sprouts
  • Chilli (sliced thinly)
  • Thai Basil
  • Mint
  • Lime (wedges)

Method

Part 1 - Broth

  1. Soak Banh Pho in cold water to rehydrate - set aside
  2. Bring the hot water to a low simmer
  3. Cut the apple, pear and carrot into ‚Öì and add to the water. *Make sure you remove the core
  4. Add shiitake mushrooms
  5. Cut leek into ‚Öì and add the green bits to the broth. Finely chop the white part and set aside *we will use this part to stir fry with the mushrooms and broccoli leaves
  6. Char ginger, onion, shallots and daikon
  7. Let it rest into a cold bowl of water then scrape off the black char
  8. Add to the broth
  9. Toast your spices, rinse them then add to the broth *your senses will let you know when it‚Äôs time
  10. Add 2 tablespoons of sea salt and the rock sugar
  11. Let the broth simmer for ~1hr *Pro Tip: Leave it uncovered
  12. Remove all the veggies from the pot (keep the Shiitake Mushrooms and ginger)
  13. Use a fine filter to fish out the spices in the broth
  14. Add ginger back to the broth and season it with soy sauce/salt
  15. Let it simmer while you prep the stir fry

Part 2 - Veggie/Tofu Stir-Fry

  1. In a hot wok add sesame oil (or water) and in about ¬Ω the white leek. Stir fry it for 30 seconds
  2. Add in mushrooms and stir fry until desired texture (you can also add in some soy sauce)
  3. Reuse the wok and add in the rest of the leek
  4. Add in the stalks of the chinese broccoli as it takes longer to cook. *I usually cook it for about 1-1¬Ω minutes. Then add in the leaves with a touch of water, chuck on the lid and let it steam
  5. Remove from wok and use a paper towel to absorb any excess water
  6. Cut tofu into strips and place them in the hot wok to toast
  7. Once toasted, turn the heat off then start preparing the garnishes/assembling your bowl

Part 3 - Garnishes

  1. Wash the garnishes
  2. Roughly chopped coriander + green onion

Part 4 - Assembly

  1. Cook noodles in salted boiling water, once cooked, drain under cold water to prevent sticking
  2. Add a handful of noodles to your bowl topped with a sprinkle of the coriander/green onion mix and 2 ladles of soup
  3. Layer veggie/tofu stir fry and shiitake mushrooms then add 1 more ladle of soup
  4. Finish it off with some sprouts, chill mint, thai basil and lime
